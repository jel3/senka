# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

## Summary

- Be respectful and welcoming to all contributors regardless of background or experience level.
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- No harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to <jel3.dev@gmail.com>. All reports will be handled confidentially. Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions and to ban contributors who do not follow this code of conduct.
